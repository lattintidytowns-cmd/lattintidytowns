Lattin Tidy Town

    Tidy town history

    Lattin Tidy Towns was established in 2012 and it has entered the Tidy Towns Competition every year since. Marks awarded in 2012 were 263 and we have come up the marks over the years to score 353 marks in the 2025 competition.Numerous improvements have been completed since 2012 with new footpaths, pedestrian crossing, flower beds, signage for roads and the River Ara and Tumulus, street furniture, refurbishment of a phone box, painting etc. There is a renewed pride in the parish with the completion and maintenance of these works. There is a very good Tennis Club and GAA Club in the village. All this fosters a great sense of community in the village which has a population of approx. 100 and our volunteers strive to continue with improvements each year.
