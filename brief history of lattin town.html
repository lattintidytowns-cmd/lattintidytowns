    Lattin Tidy Town

    BRIEF HISTORY OF LATTIN

    The village comprises of a church, two cemeteries and two pubs. On the sports grounds there is a hall for meetings and for amateur shows (school concerts, Scór competitions etc.). The cemetery is located approximately 100 metres west of the church. One pub is on the corner opposite the cemetery's entrance. The other is located on the other side of the village, on the road leading to Tipperary town.
